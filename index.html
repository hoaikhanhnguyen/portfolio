About me

Welcome to my website!

My name is Khanh and I graduated with a degree in exercise science and sport marketing from Rutgers University in New Brunswick, NJ.

In high school, I took basic programming classes, learning basic html, C, and Java. At Rutgers, I completed several engineering classes and got some experience with Matlab and Maple.

These skills helped me create content for my sports blogs. Being a big stats guy, I organized a pickup basketball league where the players' stats were recorded, converted into fantasy points, and posted on the league website

Web Developer, Sports Fan, and Competitive Gaming Enthusiast

As many programmers experience, I hit a wall with my programming knowledge. Lacking the resources to overcome it, I took some time off to focus on other life goals such as volunteering to help run the world's largest fighting game tournament (EVO) and hiking the Grand Canyon.

Life would eventually take me back to programming, as I began researching ways to create an eSports website. This time around, with proper guidance from friends and mentors, I was able to push myself further than ever. Recreating classic games and building a database of over 100,000 matches are among some of the projects that have fueled my passion to code. Being able to create projects that are only limited by my imagination is exhilarating!
